About Ferengi Game Library

As a team, we have created the Ferengi Game Library so that users can enjoy a variety of game titles, compete to earn high scores on our imported options, as well as send us their own open source links to be reviewed and possibly seen deployed along side our original library. Our aim as developers was to create something that users would enjoy, as well as gaining experience in web-developement.
